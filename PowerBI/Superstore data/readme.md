# Superstore Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## Overview

Interactive Power BI dashboard built on the Superstore dataset.  
The project analyzes sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across U.S. states, product categories, and time periods.

Dataset:  
https://www.kaggle.com/code/tarekmasryo/data-analysis-for-superstore-dataset

---

## Key Features

### Geographic Analysis
- Map visualization with proportional bubbles  
- Darker and larger bubbles indicate higher order volume  
- Quick identification of high-demand states  

### Core KPIs
- Unique Products  
- Unique Customers  
- Unique Orders  
- Total Profit  

### Profitability Analysis
- Profit by Sub-Category (horizontal bar chart)  
- Profit by Category  
- Category Sales Share  

### Customer & Trend Insights
- Top Customers by Profit  
- Yearly Sales Trend  

---

## Interactivity

- Filter by Year and Month  
- Cross-filtering via chart interaction  
- Dynamic KPI updates

## Business Value

- Regional performance insights  
- Product portfolio evaluation
